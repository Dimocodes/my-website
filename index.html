Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken Fried Rice is a flavorful and colorful dish made with rice, vegetables, and seasoned chicken. It is perfect for parties, family meals, and special occasions.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 5 people

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, sweet corn)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Curry powder

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken with salt, curry, and seasoning cubes.
  2. Cook the chicken until tender and set aside.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan and fry chopped onions.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  5. Add the cooked rice and mix well with the vegetables.
  6. Add the cooked chicken and stir until everything is well combined.
  7. Cook for another 5 minutes and serve hot.

Tips

Use chicken stock instead of water to cook the rice for a richer flavor.
